About Me

I am from Rothesay, NB, Canada where I call home in the summer and during the rest of the year I am pro hockey player. For the 2022-2023 season I am playing in the top Finnish hockey league called Liiga for KalPa. I graduated from the University of New Brunswick in 2020 with a Bachelor of Computer Science. I also played on the UNB Men's Hockey team from 2016-2020.

I am currently building my software developement portfolio on the side while playing hockey with aspirations of doing some part time work on the side while playing pro hockey.
